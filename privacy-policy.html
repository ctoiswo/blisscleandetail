Last updated: May 3, 2026

BlissClean Mobile Detail, LLC ("BlissClean," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our mobile car detailing services.

We use the information we collect to:

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and for analytics purposes. We use:

You can control cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner or your browser settings. Declining optional cookies will not affect your ability to use our services.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
